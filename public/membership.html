Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time through the "Manage Billing" button. You'll continue to have access to your plan features until the end of your current billing period.
How do I upgrade or change my plan?
You can upgrade, downgrade, or change your plan at any time through the "Manage Billing" button. This will take you to our secure customer portal where you can make changes to your subscription.
What happens when I change my plan?
When you upgrade, you'll immediately get access to all the features of your new plan. When you downgrade, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing period. We'll prorate any changes appropriately.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and debit cards through our secure payment processor, Stripe.
Is there a refund policy?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.
